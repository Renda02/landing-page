more about rainy 4G

rain’s 4G products are all about making data both affordable and unlimited. We offer a choice of plans to suit your needs, from unlimited off-peak for only R250 a month to anytime unlimited for R479 a month.

If you’ve got a 4G-enabled phone, tablet or MiFi device, you can use rain 4G. We’ve got coverage in most towns and metros across South Africa, and we won’t ask you to sign a contract.
